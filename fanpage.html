The Neighbourhood Fanpage

The Neighbourhood consists of five current members, originating from Newbury Park, California. The band is composed of vocalist Jesse Rutherford, guitarists Zach Abels and Jeremy Freedman, bassist Mikey Margott, and drummer Brandon Alexander Fried. They have two studio albums and one mixtape released up to date.

They are well known for their distinct sound, addicting compositions, and raw lyrics. Mostly known for their Billboard Chart topper "Sweater Weather", the group has steadily built it's following and is currently touring to support it's album "Wiped Out".
